## Format Observations

- **Duration range**: 21 seconds to 44 minutes — huge variance
- **Sweet spots**: Very short (<1 min) for quick hits, 2-10 min for explainers, 15-25 min for deep tutorials
- **Article format** (图文) exists (our 1 post uses it) but much less common
- **Video dominates** — even "articles" tend to be video-first
- **Hashtag strategy matters**: #AI #AIGC #AI新星计划 #大模型 are common; Douyin campaign tags like #抖音年味新知贺岁 #前沿科技趋势发布月 give algorithm boost
- **Related search terms** for "AI教程": ai小程序入口, 从零开始学ai人工智能, ai一键生成入口, ai视频生成教程
- **Related search terms** for "AI资讯": ai资讯最前沿, ai行业最新资讯, ai科技最前线

## What Stands Out

1. **The tutorial space is crowded** — everyone teaches AI tools. The top accounts have established authority and massive libraries.
2. **Weekly news roundups have reliable views** — @产品君 consistently gets 2-6万 views with a formulaic weekly format.
3. **Deep analysis is underserved** — fewer people doing it, and when done well (like @晓辉博士's Agent trend piece at 7567 views), it punches above its weight for the niche.
4. **Douyin-native AI tools** (即梦, 豆包, 剪映) are a massive search category — people searching for these specifically.
5. **"Information gap" framing** is extremely popular — "打破信息差" appears repeatedly in successful titles.
6. **Our account already follows many AI creators** — the recommendation algorithm likely already classifies us as AI-interested, which could help with initial distribution.
7. **The 1 AI post we have (article format, 63 views) performed poorly** compared to average AI content. Likely because: article format, no visual hook, no hashtag strategy, no consistency.
