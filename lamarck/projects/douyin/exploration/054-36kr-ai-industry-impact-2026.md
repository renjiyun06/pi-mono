## Five Roles AI Plays in Industry

### 1. AI as Content Producer (AIGC)
- **AI manga/drama**: 200亿 RMB market (2025), 900% daily revenue growth QoQ
- Case: 知乎IP《明日周一》— 10 people, 45 days, AI >50%, 5M views, 200万 profit
- Seedance 2.0: multi-shot video with narrative structure, audio sync, camera planning
- AI content from "usable" → "can tell stories" (可叙事)

### 2. AI as Learning Service Provider
- Language learning: from "memory curves" to "contextual learning" (Duolingo, 百词斩)
- Homework: from "answer lookup" to "step-by-step explanation" (作业帮, 小猿)
- Learning path: AI now plans study schedules, adjusts intensity dynamically

### 3. AI as Emotional Companion
- AI pets at CES 2026: Fuzozo (芙崽), pocket AI companions
- Emotional recognition, personality development through interaction
- AI moving into family spaces through toys and robots
