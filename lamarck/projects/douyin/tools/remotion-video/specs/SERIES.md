## Publishing Strategy

**Phase 1: Test with Series 1** — Post 4 confession videos over 2 weeks. Measure:
- Completion rate (> 40% = good for 50s+ videos)
- Comment quality (emotional vs factual)
- Share rate (> 5% = strong social currency)

**Phase 2: Alternate Series 1 + Series 2** — If confession works, add humor to show range.

**Phase 3: Full rotation** — Once we have data, rotate across all 4 series.
